About Me

Raymond Mutua is a Kenyan fashion model and brand influencer who has earned international recognition through pageantry and athleticism. He currently holds the titles of Mr. Co-operative University of Kenya 2025/2026 and Mr. Birmingham Ambassador Kenya, representing youth leadership and cultural exchange. His signature look and professional demeanor have made him a sought‑after face for editorial shoots, runway shows and commercial campaigns.

An accomplished athlete, Raymond captains the rugby team at the Co-operative University of Kenya, leading by example on the field with discipline, strategic thinking and sportsmanship. He balances rigorous training with academic studies, demonstrating dedication to both fitness and education. His leadership extends to mentoring younger teammates and organizing community outreach events.

Off the field and off the runway, Raymond is also an avid swimmer and public speaker, frequently invited to speak at youth conferences and fashion forums. He believes in using his platform to inspire confidence, promote health, and encourage the next generation of models and athletes.
